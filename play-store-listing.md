```
Working hourly in Greece? MyShift helps you know exactly what you're owed.

Log your shifts and the app automatically calculates your net pay based on Greek labor law — premiums, insurance, bonuses, everything.

━━━ WHO IS IT FOR ━━━

• Waiters & baristas
• Warehouse & logistics workers
• Cleaners
• Delivery & courier riders
• Retail & supermarket employees
• Every hourly worker in Greece

━━━ WHAT IT DOES ━━━

★ SHIFT LOGGING
Enter start time, end time, break, and day type. Pick a preset (morning/afternoon/night) or create your own.

★ AUTOMATIC CALCULATION
See your earnings INSTANTLY as you fill in the form — gross, insurance, net.

★ ALL LEGAL PREMIUMS
• Overwork (9th hour): +20%
• Overtime (10th+ hour): +40% or +60% (>150h/year)
• Night work (22:00-06:00): +25%
• Sunday / Public Holiday: +75%
• Saturday: +30% (fixed schedule) or +40% (rotating)
Premiums stack cumulatively — exactly as the law requires!

★ INSURANCE BY CATEGORY
• Regular income: 13.37% (IKA/EFKA)
• Overtime income: 6.37%
• Premiums: 0% — Exempt (Law 5184/2025 Art.35)

★ GROSS / NET TOGGLE
Enter your hourly rate as gross or net — the app converts automatically.

★ BONUSES & ALLOWANCES
• Christmas Bonus (max 25 daily wages)
• Easter Bonus (max 15 daily wages)
• Vacation Allowance (max 13 daily wages)
Step-by-step calculation based on your actual working days.

★ REPORTS & EXPORT
Monthly & yearly reports, hours chart, export to PDF & CSV.

★ CALENDAR
View your shifts on a monthly calendar with Greek public holidays marked (14 holidays including Orthodox Easter).

★ LEGAL INFO
Easy-to-understand section about your rights — with law references:
• Law 4808/2021 (overwork, overtime)
• Law 5184/2025 Art.35 (insurance exemption)
• Law 5053/2023 (Saturday rotating schedule)
• Government Gazette 1476/27-3-2025 (minimum €5.28/hour)

━━━ FEATURES ━━━

✓ 100% offline — works without internet
✓ Cloud backup (optional)
✓ Dark mode
✓ Greek & English
✓ Reminder notifications
✓ Standalone calculator (no entry required)
✓ Minimum wage April 2025: €880/month

━━━ SEPE ━━━
If you're facing issues at work, call the Labor Inspectorate: 1555

This app does not replace an accountant — it is an informational tool.

Questions? fiouri.work@gmail.com
```
